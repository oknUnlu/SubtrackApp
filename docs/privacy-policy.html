SubTrack - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Summary: SubTrack stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Introduction

SubTrack ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal expense and subscription tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data Collection & Storage

SubTrack does not collect any personal data. All information you enter into the app, including:

  • Expense records and transaction history
  • Subscription details
  • Budget settings
  • App preferences (currency, language, theme)

is stored locally on your device only using an on-device SQLite database. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Analytics & Insights

The spending analysis and smart tips features in SubTrack operate entirely on your device. No data is sent to external AI services or cloud servers for processing. All calculations are performed locally.

4. Advertising

SubTrack uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information and advertising identifiers to serve ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We have configured ads to request non-personalized ads only by default.

Information that AdMob may collect includes:

  • Device advertising identifier
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address (for approximate location-based ads)

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob - For displaying advertisements (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo - For app updates and delivery (Privacy Policy)

6. Permissions

SubTrack may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications - To send subscription renewal reminders (optional)
  • Biometric Authentication - To protect app access with fingerprint/face (optional)
  • Internet Access - Required for displaying advertisements

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the app's Settings page using the "Delete All Data" option. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data from your device.

8. Children's Privacy

SubTrack is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
